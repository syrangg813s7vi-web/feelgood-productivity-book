Title: Feeling Lost? Try These Journaling Prompts
Video ID: rkorr-tHPTM
URL: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rkorr-tHPTM
Author: Ali Abdaal

================================================================================

These are five journaling prompts that you can use if you are ever feeling a little lost in life. Number one, what would I like to do if I knew I wouldn't fail? Number two, what would I choose to do even if I knew I would fail? Number three, if I could design my absolutely perfect, ideal, ordinary Tuesday, what would it look like? Number four, what is the TED talk that I would really like to be giving 30 years from now? Number five, in this moment, what is actually wrong?
