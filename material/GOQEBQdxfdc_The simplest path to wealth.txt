This is a book that you should send to your friends if they are interested in investing their money. It is called The Simple Path to Wealth by JL Collins. I read this book 10 years ago back in 2015 and this was the book that got me first interested in investing. I'd been sort of interested in a while, but I was always scared. I thought I might lose all my money. I didn't know how it worked. But then I read this book and it completely changed my life. So, if you have that friend who wants to invest their money but is afraid and doesn't know how to do it, then you should totally sell send them the book Simple Path to Wealth by JL Collins.
