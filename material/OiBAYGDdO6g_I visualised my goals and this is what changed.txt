One of the most productive things I did in 2025 was to create a vision board. This is what my vision board looks like. I set this at the start of the year and I built it in like a Figma file or whatever. And this has been my desktop wallpaper since that time. And this is great. Like it's just given me a lot of clarity on what I actually want. So I've got this guy over here. He's a martial artist. So you know for the first 6 months of the year I was doing Mai. I had this goal to become like a really good squash player. So I took a bunch of squash lessons and then like 6 months into the year I decided actually I don't really want to do that. I've got a few pictures here of like these are like the apps that we're building. You know, this is a photo of the house that I would like to have in the future. This is a picture of like a random woman with three kids, which signifies that I would like to have at least three kids, maybe four, maybe five, but like we'll see what my wife has to say about that. And just having this vision board visible on my desktop wallpaper every day is actually quite motivating and quite inspiring as a reminder for me of like these are the sorts of things I want to do. And so when I see like the jacked dude doing high kick or whatever, or like the guy playing squash, you know, it reminds me I haven't been to the gym today, so I should probably go to the gym today. So if you don't yet have a vision board, I think it's really helpful to have one and to set it as your desktop wallpaper.
