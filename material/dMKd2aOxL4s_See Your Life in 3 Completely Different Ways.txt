If you are struggling to figure out what to do with your life, there is a technique from the guys who wrote this book, Bill Bernard and Dave Evans. And the technique is called the Odyssey Plan. Basically, the way it works is that you set a 10-minute timer and you ask yourself the question of if I continued down my current path for the next 5 years, where would I end up? What would a day in my life look like? And so, you write that out in a bunch of detail. Then, when you've done that, you set another 10-minute timer and you ask yourself, if I were to take a completely different path, where would my life end up 5 years from now? The first one is your current path. The second one is your alternative path. And then once you've done that, you ask yourself a third time. You set a timer for 10 minutes. This is your radical path. You ask yourself, what would my life look like 5 years from now if I took a completely different path where I didn't care about money and I did not care about what other people thought about me? This is an amazing exercise that I talk about in page 242 of my book, Feel Good Productivity, as well. It's completely changed my life.
