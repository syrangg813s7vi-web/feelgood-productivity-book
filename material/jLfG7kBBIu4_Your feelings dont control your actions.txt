Title: Your feelings don't control your actions
Video ID: jLfG7kBBIu4
URL: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jLfG7kBBIu4
Author: Ali Abdaal

================================================================================

One thing that's really helped me in my life is recognizing that thoughts, feelings, and actions do not actually have to be correlated. Like back in the day, I used to think that like if I don't feel like doing a thing, then I shouldn't do the thing. Like I don't feel like going to the gym. The thought is like, h I don't really want to go to the gym. And then the action is not going to the gym. But I've realized over the years, especially as I built my own business and had to do stuff that I didn't feel like a lot of the time. It's totally okay to have the thought, I don't want to go to the gym. It's also totally okay to not actually feel like going to the gym. And it's also totally okay at the same time to actually go to the gym. The thought, the feeling, and the action does not have to correlate. You are in control. You're in charge. You can decide if you would like the thought, the feeling, and the action to correlate, which is fine. You can also decide if you don't want to have the thought, feeling, and action correlate. The thought, the feeling, and the action are like three separate sliders, and they're actually completely unrelated to each other. Whereas the way most of us operate is that if one goes up, the others have to go up. My actions must line up with my thoughts and my feelings. And this is actually a myth.
