Okay, so we've got less than 10 days left in 2025. Here are three questions that you might like to ask yourself during the holiday period. The first one is, if you were in a movie of your life, what would the audience be screaming at you to do? What's the one thing that you know you should do, but you're just not doing it? Like, what's what's that thing? Secondly, if you were to fast forward to the end of 2026, what are three things in your work and three things in your life that you would love to have accomplished by that time? And thirdly, I'd recommend asking yourself that if everything else about your life were to stay exactly the same, what is the one area where changing something would have the biggest impact? Those are some good questions to ask.
