How do I become the first millionaire in my family? Okay. Well, how do you become a millionaire? How do you have a net worth of over a million dollars? There's a way I like to think about this, which is that if you want to make, let's say, a million dollars, you [music] have to add more than a million dollars to other people's lives. Because the only way to make money is for someone else to give you that money in return for something that they value less than they value giving the money, if that makes sense. like, you know, the only reason I bought an iPhone is because I value having an iPhone more than I value the $1,000 or so that the iPhone cost. Similarly, the only way for you to make a million dollars is to add more than a million dollars of value to other people's lives. And then the two vehicles for adding this kind of value is either you do it through a job or you do it through a business. Now, if you're asking the question of how you become the first millionaire in your family, you probably don't have a job that lends itself to becoming very rich. So, if you're asking the question, then you probably want to aim towards trying to become a millionaire through starting your own business. People get very scared of starting their own business. [music] But a business is basically a vehicle that provides value to other people and that's wrapped around this legal thing that we call business, which is really not that scary and not that hard. We just need to find something, some kind of skill or expertise that we have or that we can learn that will add lots of value to someone else's life. Easiest way to add value to someone else's life is to help them make money. So, for example, the people that get the richest tend to be the people that help businesses make more money. So, if you could learn a high income skill that helps a business make money, you can build [music] a business around that skill. Lots more to say on this, but the best book I've ever read about this is called The Millionaire Fast Lane by MJ DeMarco. Bit of a clickbay title, but it's genuinely freaking sick. So if you actually want to become a millionaire, 100% read the book, The Millionaire Fastly.
